Credits

This site is built with the static site generator Hugo, and uses a customised theme based on Anatole by Alexander Bilz, itself based on farbox-theme-Anatole by Cai Cai. The paginator is based on an article by Glenn McComb.

The fonts are Roboto by Christian Robertson and Open Sans by Steve Matteson, both from Google Fonts.

The glitch image was made with the fantastic iOS app Retrospecs, and the Hugo shortcode used to embed Mastodon Toots is by Bryce Wray.

After many years of being hosted on AWS S3, the site now lives on GitHub.
