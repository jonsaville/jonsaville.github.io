Happy New Year 2024

1 January 2024    sitenew year   Permalink

Okay so this is a cheat - I’m writing this in May 2024, in the Highlands of Scotland, in glorious sunshine, shortly after the amazing aurora display and shortly before my fiftieth birthday.

The last 18 months have been hectic to say the least, but a post on Mastodon reminded me not to worry too much about this site.

I have a few posts queued so we’ll see how quickly they are published. The rest of 2024 promises to be busy as well, so in the meantime I am enjoying this beautiful part of the world with Lindsey, drinking a little whisky, and not worrying too much about the outside world.
