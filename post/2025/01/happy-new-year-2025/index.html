Happy New Year 2025

1 January 2025    sitenew year   Permalink

Another year, another post.

2024 was a big year for my family - my sister got married, my parents downsized from the house I grew up in, and I turned 50 during a holiday in the Scottish Highlands. That holiday coincided with the solar storm in May, and we were treated to a spectacular aurora display in spectacular surroundings. 2025 promises to be just as busy.

The end of the year also saw some IT spring cleaning. I moved my email from MS365 to Proton Mail - the gradual AI enshittification of Microsoft has just got too much. After many years of using Adobe Lightroom, I cancelled that subscription as well and I’m migrating nearly 100,000 photographs to Digikam.

I’ve been using bookmarking services since 2008, and for the last 10 years that service has been Pinboard. It’s proprietor has an admirable approach to keeping things tight and profitable. Unfortunately over the last few years Pinboard increasingly feels like abandonware. I can tolerate a lack of convenience features, but API outages are becoming more frequent. I’m switching to Raindrop.io which has felt like a breath of fresh air, with decent first-party apps and all the modern features you would expect. Perhaps Pinboard will outlive this service as well, but I’ve realised that life is short, so perhaps it’s better to enjoy a superior experience at the expense of longevity.

I’ve also had a good time moving from Twitter to Mastodon, specifically the Hachyderm instance. It’s not perfect, but suits me very well and feels like an earlier, less terrible version of the Internet. As a result I took the penultimate step of deleting all my tweets. Perhaps in the future Twitter will become a useful resource again, but again: why wait?

For this site I’d like to update the style to reflect modern practices, and who knows, even post a little.
